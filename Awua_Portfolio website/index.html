About Dr. Awua

Dr. Awua Justin Tarka is a Senior Lecturer in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Joseph Sarwuan Tarka University, Makurdi, Nigeria, where he currently serves as the Postgraduate Students Coordinator.

He earned his Ph.D in Mechanical Engineering (Thermofluid & Energy Studies) from Joseph Sarwuan Tarka University in 2023, building on an M.Eng from Federal University of Agriculture Makurdi and a B.Eng (Second Class Honours Upper Division) from the same institution.

Beyond academia, Dr. Awua has worked at the highest levels of Nigerian industry — from Shell's Bonga Northwest offshore project to plant valuation consulting for multinational corporations across Nigeria.
